## General Presentation

- Role: [[Controller]]
- Origin: United Kingdom  
- Playstyle: Crowd control / Area denial  
- Strengths: Smokes, zoning, delaying enemy pushes  

Clove is a tactical controller who manipulates the battlefield with elemental orbs. She slows, blocks, and disrupts opponents to allow her team to safely take control of sites.

## How to Play Clove

### Attacking
- Use **Smoke Orb** to block lines of sight for safe entries  
- **Shatter** to weaken defenders in tight angles  
- **Magnetize** to slow rotations or delay bomb plants  
- **Overload** for post-plant control and zone disruption  

### Defending
- Pre-place **Magnetize** zones in high-traffic areas  
- Smoke key choke points with **Smoke Orb**  
- Shatter enemies attempting to push through  
- Ultimate can turn the tide in late-round site defense  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy **controlling enemy movement and vision**  
- Controllers who provide both offensive and defensive utility  
- Strategic thinkers who plan zone control in advance
