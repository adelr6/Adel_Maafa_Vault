## General Presentation

- Role: [[Duelist]]
- Origin: Thailand  
- Playstyle: High mobility / Speed & repositioning  
- Strengths: Fast repositioning, debuff utility, unpredictable movement

Waylay is a Radiant Duelist specialized in manipulating light for incredible mobility, strategic repositioning, and slowing enemy reactions with her unique abilities. 

---

## Abilities

| Icon                      | Ability          | Type      | Description                                                                                                                                  |
| ------------------------- | ---------------- | --------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[Saturate.png]]         | Saturate         | Basic     | Throws a cluster of light that explodes on the ground, slowing movement and hindering weapons for affected enemies.                          |
| ![[Lightspeed.png]]       | Light Speed      | Basic     | Equips and performs up to two quick dashes forward; alt-fire performs one dash, and only the first can go upward.                            |
| ![[Refract.png]]          | Refract          | Signature | Instantly places a beacon; reactivate to teleport back to it as a mote of light, becoming invulnerable while traveling.                      |
| ![[Convergent_Paths.png]] | Convergent Paths | Ultimate  | Equips to create an afterimage that projects a beam; after a short delay you gain speed and the beam expands, hindering enemies in its area. |

---

## How to Play Waylay

### Attacking
- Use Light Speed to swiftly enter fights and confuse defenders  
- Place a Refract beacon before committing, so you have a safe return option  
- Saturate can hinder rotations and slow enemy peeks  
- Convergent Paths provides both a speed boost and area slow during executes

### Defending
- Hold angles then dash out for sudden reposition  
- Use Saturate to delay pushes and make enemies easy targets  
- Drop a Refract beacon before mid fights for escape paths  
- Activate Convergent Paths to slow pushes and control space

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who like fast-paced impact  
- Those who enjoy creative mobility  
- Duelists that thrive on repositioning and outplays
