## General Presentation

- Role: [[Controller]]
- Origin: Ghana  
- Playstyle: Space control / Crowd disruption  
- Strengths: Vision denial, site control, team support  

Astra manipulates the battlefield from afar using stars that can be placed anywhere on the map. Her abilities allow her to slow, concuss, smoke, or block lines of sight to support her team strategically.

---

## Abilities

| Icon                   | Ability                     | Type     | Description                                                                                                                 |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------- | -------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[gravity_well.png]]  | Gravity Well                | Basic    | Activates a star to pull in enemies and make them vulnerable to attacks.                                                    |
| ![[nova_pulse.png]]    | Nova Pulse                  | Basic    | Triggers a concussive pulse at a star location to disorient enemies.                                                        |
| ![[nebula.png]]        | Nebula                      | Basic    | Converts a star into a smoke to block vision and control space.                                                             |
| ![[cosmic divide.png]] | Astral Form / Cosmic Divide | Ultimate | Enters Astral Form to place stars, or creates a massive wall (Cosmic Divide) that blocks bullets and heavily reduces sound. |

---

## How to Play Astra

### Attacking
- Place stars to block vision and create safe paths  
- Use Gravity Well or Nova Pulse to disrupt defenders  
- Convert stars to smokes for controlled pushes  
- Cosmic Divide to execute site control and block enemy sightlines  

### Defending
- Pre-place stars in common push paths  
- Use Nova Pulse and Gravity Well to slow attackers  
- Nebula to obscure vision and deny bomb plants  
- Cosmic Divide to lock down sites during post-plant situations  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy **strategic map control and team coordination**  
- Controllers who plan abilities in advance for maximum impact  
- Those who like **area denial and high utility gameplay**
