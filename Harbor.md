## General Presentation

- Role: [[Controller]]
- Origin: India  
- Playstyle: Area denial / Crowd control  
- Strengths: Water-based zoning, smokes, slowing enemies  

Harbor controls the battlefield using water-themed abilities to block vision, slow enemies, and control space for his team.

---

## Abilities

| Icon                 | Ability   | Type      | Description                                                                                             |
| -------------------- | --------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[storm_surge.png]] | Cascade   | Basic     | Creates an explosive whirlpool that Slows and Nearsights enemies within it after a short duration.      |
| ![[cove.png]]        | Cove      | Basic     | Creates a water sphere that blocks vision and slows enemies inside.                                     |
| ![[high_tide.png]]   | High Tide | Signature | Summons a wall of water that moves forward, blocking sightlines and slowing enemies.                    |
| ![[reckoning.png]]   | Reckoning | Ultimate  | Unleashes a massive water surge that pulses multiple times, revealing and damaging enemies in its area. |

---

## How to Play Harbor

### Attacking
- Use **Cove** and **High Tide** to block vision for site entries  
- **Cascade** to harass or zone defenders  
- Ultimate **Reckoning** to secure a site or force enemy rotation  

### Defending
- Pre-place **Cove** and **High Tide** to slow pushes  
- **Cascade** to punish overextensions  
- Ultimate can deny a plant or retake effectively  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy **area denial and zoning**  
- Controllers who manipulate space strategically  
- Those who coordinate with teammates for maximal map control
