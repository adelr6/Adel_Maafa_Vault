#  Corrode


Corrode is a Valorant map with **two main sites (A and B)** and several mid-range lanes. The map emphasizes **flexible rotations, flanking opportunities, and strong use of utility**. Controlling chokepoints and predicting enemy movement is key to success.

---

## General Presentation

- Number of Sites: 2 (A & B)
- Special Feature: Multiple flanking paths and chokepoints
- Typical Playstyle: Flexible, utility-heavy, rotation-focused
- Good for: Duelists, Initiators, Controllers, supportive Sentinels

---

## Key Areas of the Map

### Site A
- Open plant zones with multiple entry points
- Great for flanking or fast entry plays
- Works well with utility-heavy agents

### Site B
- Tighter corridors with long angles
- Strong for controlling chokepoints
- Ideal for coordinated pushes

### Mid
- Central connector between both sites
- Critical for rotations and early picks
- Can be dominated by agile or vision-heavy agents

---

## Strategic Approach

### Attacking on Corrode
- Take control of Mid to split defenders
- Use flanking paths for surprise attacks
- Coordinate utility to secure sites

Recommended attackers:
- [[Phoenix]]  
- [[Raze]]  
- [[Yoru]]  

---

### Defending on Corrode
- Hold key chokepoints
- Use utility to delay attackers
- Rotate efficiently between A and B
