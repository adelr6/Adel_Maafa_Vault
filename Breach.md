## General Presentation

- Role: [[Initiator]]
- Origin: Sweden  
- Playstyle: Aggressive / Crowd control  
- Strengths: Stuns, area disruption, entry facilitation  

Breach excels at breaking through defensive setups. His abilities stun, displace, and damage enemies, making him perfect for coordinated site entries.

---

## Abilities

| Icon                     | Ability         | Type      | Description                                                                              |
| ------------------------ | --------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[aftershock.png]]      | Aftershock      | Basic     | Fires a charge through a wall that deals damage in a cone.                               |

---

## How to Play Breach

### Attacking
- Use Flashpoint to blind enemies before peeking  
- Aftershock clears tight positions and delays rotations  
- Fault Line sets up easy kills or forces repositioning  
- Rolling Thunder opens sites for team entry  

### Defending
- Stun pushes with Fault Line and Flashpoint  
- Use Aftershock to punish aggressors  
- Ultimate can deny plant attempts or retake sites  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who like coordinated, high-impact plays  
- Initiators that enjoy breaking enemy defenses  
- Aggressive team players
