# Viper

Viper is a Controller who specializes in **area denial, zone control, and post-plant utility**. She uses poison-based abilities that deal decay damage, forcing enemies to make bad decisions and slowing pushes significantly.

---

## General Presentation

- Role: [[Controller]]
    
- Country: USA
    
- Playstyle: Zone control / Tactical
    
- Very strong in: Site control, choke locking, post-plant
    

Viper is perfect for players who like to control space, manipulate enemy movement, and play smart rather than purely aggressive.

## How to Play Viper

### Attacking

- Use Toxic Screen to cut sites in half
    
- Poison Cloud is perfect for covering plant positions
    
- Snake Bite is extremely strong in post-plant
    
- Play smarter, not faster: control space and force enemies into bad duels
    

**Great maps to attack with Viper:**

- Bind
    
- Breeze
    
- Icebox
    

---

### Defending

- Block main choke points with wall or smoke
    
- Delay pushes instead of always fighting
    
- Use utility to stop plants or deny space
    
- Save Snake Bite for post-plant whenever possible
    

**Very strong defensive maps:**

- Bind
    
- Icebox
    
- Lotus
    

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who like controlling the battlefield
    
- Strategic and patient players
    
- Those who enjoy post-plant lineups
    

---
