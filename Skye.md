## General Presentation

- Role: [[Initiator]]
- Origin: Australia  
- Playstyle: Support / Vision control  
- Strengths: Healing, flashes, scouting  

Skye excels at supporting her team with heals, flashes, and information-gathering. Her abilities make pushing, retaking, and controlling areas safer and more predictable.

---

## Abilities

| Icon                   | Ability       | Type      | Description                                                                                                                       |
| ---------------------- | ------------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[Regrowth.png]]      | Regrowth      | Basic     | Heals all nearby allies over time.                                                                                                |
| ![[Trailblazer.png]]   | Trailblazer   | Basic     | Sends out a controllable Tasmanian tiger that explodes on impact, damaging and concussing enemies.                                |
| ![[Guiding_Light.png]] | Guiding Light | Signature | Equips a hawk that can be thrown to flash enemies in a line of sight.                                                             |
| ![[Seekers.png]]       | Seekers       | Ultimate  | Sends out three seekers that track the three nearest enemies, revealing their location and applying nearsight if they are caught. |

---

## How to Play Skye

### Attacking
- Use Guiding Light to flash before entering sites  
- Trailblazer to clear corners and force rotations  
- Regrowth keeps teammates alive during pushes  
- Seekers to locate hidden defenders  

### Defending
- Scout areas with Trailblazer or Seekers  
- Flash enemies to stop pushes  
- Heal teammates to maintain site control  
- Coordinate ultimate with team for retakes  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Supportive players who like coordinating with the team  
- Those who enjoy vision and utility control  
- Players who can multitask abilities and aim
