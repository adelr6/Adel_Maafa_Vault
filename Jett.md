## Role & Playstyle

- Role: [[Duelist]]
- Playstyle: Agile, entry-fragger, high mobility  
- Focus: Quick kills, creating space for the team, surprise plays  

---

| ![[cloudburst.png]] | Cloudburst | Throw a quick smoke cloud to block vision. Can curve around corners.    |     |
| ![[updraft.png]]    | Updraft    | Propel yourself upward to reach high ground or surprise enemies.        |     |
| ![[tailwind.png]]   | Tailwind   | Dash in the direction you are moving for quick repositioning or escape. |     |

## Strategy & Tips

- Use mobility to create unpredictable angles  
- Combine Cloudburst and Tailwind for aggressive entry  
- Take high ground with Updraft for advantage  
- Bladestorm is ideal for eco rounds or finishing fights
