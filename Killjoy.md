## General Presentation

- Role: [[Sentinel]]
- Origin: Germany  
- Playstyle: Area control / Defensive setup  
- Strengths: Zone denial, information gathering, post-plant control  

Killjoy specializes in locking down areas and gathering information for her team. Her utility makes it difficult for enemies to take control of sites.

---

## Abilities

| Icon               | Ability   | Type      | Description                                                                                       |
| ------------------ | --------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[nanoswarm.png]] | Nanoswarm | Basic     | Deploys a grenade that explodes on activation, dealing damage over time to enemies caught inside. |
| ![[alarmbot.png]]  | Alarmbot  | Basic     | Deploys a bot that hunts enemies in range, debuffing them and making them take increased damage.  |

---

## How to Play Killjoy

### Attacking
- Use Nanoswarm to clear corners or deny defuse  
- Alarmbot for information on enemy positions  
- Turret to slow enemy rotations or cover flanks  
- Lockdown to execute site control and secure kills  

### Defending
- Place Turret and Alarmbot to hold angles and gather intel  
- Nanoswarm to punish pushes or delay enemy advances  
- Lockdown to disrupt retakes or cluster enemies during post-plant  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy **area denial and strategy**  
- Those who like controlling space rather than aggressive dueling  
- Sentinels who help team coordination and post-plant control
