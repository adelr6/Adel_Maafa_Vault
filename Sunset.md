#  Sunset


Sunset is a Valorant map featuring two main sites (A and B) with medium-sized lanes and several choke points. It emphasizes mobility and quick rotations, with open spaces for mid-range fights and tight corridors for close combat.

---

## General Presentation

- Number of Sites: 2 (A & B)
- Special Feature: Combination of open areas and tight corridors
- Typical Playstyle: Aggressive pushes, map control, coordinated rotations
- Good for: Duelists, Controllers, Initiators, supportive Sentinels

---

## Key Areas of the Map

### Site A
- Wide plant zones
- Mid-range sightlines
- Good for agile entries and utility usage

### Site B
- Narrower entry points
- Close-range engagements
- Strong for defensive setups and area control

### Mid / Connector
- Small central area connecting both sites
- Control of Mid allows quick rotation and split attacks
- Aggressive agents can gain early picks

---

## Strategic Approach

### Attacking on Sunset
- Take Mid control to split defenders
- Use coordinated pushes on either site
- Exploit open areas for mobility plays

- Hold angles in chokepoints and rotate efficiently
- Use utility to delay pushes
