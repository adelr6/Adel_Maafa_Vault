## General Presentation

- Role: [[Sentinel]]
- Origin: Norway  
- Playstyle: Defensive control / Crowd disruption  
- Strengths: Crowd control, trapping enemies, area denial

Deadlock deploys cutting-edge nanowire technology to secure areas, gather intel, and slow enemies. Her toolkit lets her trap, concuss, and block off attackers. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Abilities

| Icon                  | Ability      | Type      | Description                                                                                                   |
| --------------------- | ------------ | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[sonic sensor.png]] | Sonic Sensor | Basic     | Deploys a sensor that watches for sound. If footsteps, gunfire, or other are detected, the area is concussed. |
| ![[barrier mesh.png]] | Barrier Mesh | Basic     | Throws a disc that expands into barriers blocking movement.                                                   |
| ![[Gravnet.png]]      | GravNet      | Signature | Throws a nanowire net that slows and forces enemies to crouch inside the radius.                              |

## How to Play Deadlock

### Attacking
- Deploy **Sonic Sensor** to watch potential flanks or pushes  
- Use **Barrier Mesh** to block or slow enemy rotation  
- **GravNet** to slow entries or zone control  
- **Annihilation** to catch lone enemies or clutch situations

### Defending
- Place sensors around site entries  
- Use barriers to break enemy pushes  
- Trap and slow enemies crossing chokepoints  
- Ultimate can isolate key targets or deny defuse
