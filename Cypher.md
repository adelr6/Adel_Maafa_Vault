## General Presentation

- Role: [[Sentinel]]
- Origin: Morocco  
- Playstyle: Information / Zone control  
- Strengths: Gathering intel, controlling flanks, delaying enemy pushes  

Cypher is a master of surveillance and area control, able to track enemies, trap them, and reveal their positions to his team for tactical advantage.

---

| Icon                   | Ability      | Type      | Description                                                              |
| ---------------------- | ------------ | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| ![[tripwire.png]]      | Trapwire     | Basic     | Places a tripwire that restrains and reveals enemies who cross it.       |
| ![[cyber_cage.png]]    | Cyber Cage   | Basic     | Deploys a zone that slows and obscures vision when activated.            |
| ![[spycam.png]]        | Spycam       | Signature | Places a camera to surveil an area, and can shoot a dart to tag enemies. |

---

## How to Play Cypher

### Attacking
- Place Trapwires at entry points to control rotations  
- Cyber Cage to obscure sightlines or delay enemy pushes  
- Spycam to gather intel safely before advancing  
- Neural Theft to locate enemies after a pick  

### Defending
- Use Trapwire and Spycam to monitor flanks and control sites  
- Cyber Cage to slow and disorient attackers  
- Ultimate for post-plant information and clutch setups  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy **strategic surveillance and area control**  
- Sentinels who provide information and delay tactics  
- Those who like setting traps for coordinated team play
