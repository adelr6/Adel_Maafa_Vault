## General Presentation

- Role: [[Duelist]]
- Origin: Brazil  
- Playstyle: Aggressive / Explosive  
- Strengths: High damage, chaos creation, area pressure  

Raze thrives in chaotic situations. She uses explosive abilities to flush enemies, clear corners, and control space.

## How to Play Raze

### Attacking
- Use Blast Pack to disrupt post-plant positions  
- Deploy Boom Bot to concern enemies pushing or holding tight angles  
- Paint Shells is great for clearing corners and forcing movement  
- Showstopper can win duels or deny pushes

### Defending
- Use Paint Shells to slow enemy pushes  
- Blast Pack can instantly reposition you or deal chip damage  
- Boom Bot gives enemy location info  
- Save ultimate for key moments when enemies commit

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy aggressive plays  
- Those who like dealing damage with utility  
- Players who are confident in taking duels
