## General Presentation

- Role: [[Initiator]]  
- Origin: Russia  
- Playstyle: Recon / Long-range utility  
- Strengths: Information gathering, long-range harassment, area control  

Sova excels at revealing enemies and controlling space with his recon tools and long-range abilities. Perfect for players who want to support their team with information and utility.

---

| Icon                    | Ability       | Type      | Description                                                                                             |

## How to Play Sova

### Attacking
- Use Recon Bolt to reveal defenders before pushing  
- Owl Drone for scouting tricky angles  
- Shock Bolt to clear corners or damage entrenched enemies  
- Hunter's Fury to finish off enemies or deny defuses  

### Defending
- Use Recon Bolt to gather info on attackers  
- Control mid and choke points with bolts  
- Ultimate can stop plant attempts or punish rotating enemies  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who like long-range information control  
- Those who enjoy tactical, methodical play  
- Initiators who set up the team for success
