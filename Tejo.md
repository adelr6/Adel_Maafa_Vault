## General Presentation

- Role: [[Initiator]]
- Origin: Colombia  
- Playstyle: Tactical / Recon & Area pressure  
- Strengths: Information gathering, suppression, targeted explosive control

Tejo is a veteran intelligence consultant who uses advanced drones, targeted strikes, and tactical missile barrages to pressure enemies and open opportunities for his team.

## How to Play Tejo

### Attacking
- Use **Stealth Drone** early to scout enemy positions safely  
- **Guided Salvo** to pressure chokepoints and clear angles  
- **Special Delivery** to slow or concuss defenders holding sites  
- **Armageddon** to break entrenched setups and force rotations

### Defending
- Stealth Drone can reveal enemy pushes without risking yourself  
- Special Delivery can delay aggressive entries  
- Guided Salvo locks down approach paths  
- Ultimate can deny plant attempts or punish clustered enemies

## Recommended Playstyle

- Those who want **advanced recon + area denial**
