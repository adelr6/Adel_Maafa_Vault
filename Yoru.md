## General Presentation

- Role: [[Duelist]]
- Origin: Japan  
- Playstyle: Deception / Outplay specialist  
- Strengths: Trick plays, flanks, confusion creation  

Yoru is designed for mind games. He deceives enemies, teleports, baits rotations, and punishes unaware players.

## How to Play Yoru

### Attacking
- Fake pushes to bait rotations  
- Use teleport to surprise defenders  
- Flash before taking risky duels  
- Combine Fakeout with entry timing  

### Defending
- Flank frequently  
- Create confusion during executes  
- Gather info safely with ultimate  
- Punish careless attackers

## Recommended Playstyle

- Creative players  
- Risk-takers  
- Players who enjoy deception and strategy
