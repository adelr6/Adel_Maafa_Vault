# Iso

Iso is a Duelist who focuses on **isolating enemies, winning duels, and creating 1v1 situations**. His abilities reward precision, timing, and aggressive plays, allowing him to take control of fights and snowball rounds.

---

## General Presentation

- Role: [[Duelist]]
    
- Origin: China
    
- Playstyle: Aggressive / Duel focused
    
- Strengths: Isolating duels, clutch potential, confident plays
    

---

## Abilities

| Icon                   | Ability       | Type      | Description                                                                                    |
| ---------------------- | ------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[Undercut.png]]      | Undercut      | Ability 1 | A moving energy blade that phases through walls and applies a debuff to enemies hit.           |
| ![[Contingency.png]]   | Contingency   | Ability 2 | Creates a bullet-blocking energy wall that moves forward, allowing safer pushes or peeks.      |
| ![[Double_Tap.png]]    | Double Tap    | Signature | Winning a duel creates a shield orb; grabbing it grants a temporary shield that absorbs a hit. |
| ![[Kill_Contract.png]] | Kill Contract | Ultimate  | Pulls Iso and one enemy into an isolated 1v1 arena where only one survives.                    |

---

## How to Play Iso

### Attacking

- Use Contingency to push safely into sites
    
- Take aggressive duels to activate Double Tap shield
    
- Apply pressure with Undercut to weaken defenders
    
- Use ultimate to eliminate key enemy players
    

---

### Defending

- Hold angles confidently
    
- Take calculated duels to gain shields
    
- Use Undercut to disrupt enemy entries
    
- Punish isolated attackers
    

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who love dueling
    
- Confident aimers
    
- Players who enjoy clutch and bold plays
