# Role summary

Agents under this role have abilities that allow them to focus on being the first to enter sites and engage the enemy. Unlike other roles, the signature abilities for Duelists need to be recharged with 2 kills rather than a period of time.

The game has currently 8 Duelists agents listed by chronological order of arrival : 

1. [[Phoenix]]
2. [[Raze]] (personal favorite)
3. [[Jett]]
4. [[Reyna]]
5. [[Yoru]]
6. [[Neon]]
7. [[Iso]]
8. [[Waylay]]
