## General Presentation

- Role: [[Sentinel]] 
- Origin: Senegal  
- Playstyle: Anti-utility control / Tactical disruption  
- Strengths: Utility nullification, teleport repositioning, control disruption  

Veto is a mutated Sentinel who excels at nullifying opponent utilities and controlling space with tactical tools. He can trap enemies, destroy enemy utility, teleport, and even mutate to become resilient and combat-ready. 

---

## Abilities

| Icon                 | Ability     | Type      | Description                                                                                                               |
| -------------------- | ----------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[crosscut.png]]    | Crosscut    | Basic     | Places a vortex that Veto can teleport to if he faces it and is in range. Can be reclaimed in buy phase.                  |
| ![[chokehold.png]]   | Chokehold   | Basic     | Throws a fragment that deploys a trap. Enemies caught are Deafened and Decayed, and can be trapped if they stay in range. |
| ![[interceptor.png]] | Interceptor | Signature | Deploys a device that destroys enemy utility that would bounce or be destroyed by gunfire. Enemies can destroy it.        |
| ![[evolution.png]]   | Evolution   | Ultimate  | Veto mutates fully, gaining combat stim, health regeneration, and immunity to all debuffs.                                |

---

## How to Play Veto

### Attacking
- Use **Chokehold** to trap enemies and force fights on your terms  
- **Interceptor** shuts down enemy abilities like flashes and traps  
- **Crosscut** allows repositioning or surprise rotations  
- **Evolution** lets you push aggressively without fear of crowd control 

### Defending
- Place vortexes in strategic spots for flanks or quick retreats  
- Trap choke points with Chokehold  
- Intercept enemy utility to slow their executes  
- Use Evolution to deny plant or win clutch fights 

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy **countering enemy utility**  
- Tactical defenders who want to control space  
- Those who like a mix of utility disruption and mobility
