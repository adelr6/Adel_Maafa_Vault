## General Presentation

- Role: [[Duelist]] 
- Origin: Mexico  
- Playstyle: Self-heal / Frag-dependent  
- Strengths: Snowball potential, clutch power, self-sustain  

Reyna is built for players who rely heavily on kills. Every elimination makes her stronger, allowing her to heal, escape, or dominate fights.

---

## Abilities

| Icon             | Ability | Type      | Description                                                                                         |
| ---------------- | ------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[leer.png]]    | Leer    | Basic     | Throws an ethereal eye that nearsights all enemies looking at it.                                   |
| ![[Devour.png]]  | Devour  | Basic     | Consumes a soul orb from a killed enemy to rapidly heal to full health.                             |
| ![[Dismiss.png]] | Dismiss | Signature | Consumes a soul orb to become intangible and reposition safely.                                     |
| ![[Empress.png]] | Empress | Ultimate  | Increases fire rate, reload speed, and gives automatic Devour on kills. Turns Reyna into a monster. |

---

## How to Play Reyna

### Attacking
- Take confident duels  
- Use Leer to blind defenders before peeking  
- Devour to stay alive during fights  
- Dismiss to reset fights or escape safely  

### Defending
- Take early picks then disappear  
- Use Leer to stop pushes  
- Snowball rounds if you get first blood  
- Empress to dominate in retakes or site holds  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players confident in aim  
- Solo players or frag leaders  
- Those who like 1v1 and clutch situations
