## General Presentation

- Role: [[Controller]]
- Origin: USA  
- Playstyle: Area control / Utility support  
- Strengths: Smokes, zone denial, team support  

Brimstone excels at controlling sightlines and territory using orbital technology. His abilities are perfect for blocking vision, slowing pushes, and supporting teammates from a safe distance.

---

| Icon                    | Ability        | Type      | Description                                                                             |
| ----------------------- | -------------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

---

## How to Play Brimstone

### Attacking
- Use **Sky Smoke** to block enemy sightlines for site entry  
- **Incendiary** to zone and deny plant or clear corners  
- **Stim Beacon** to buff pushes or duels  
- **Orbital Strike** to punish bomb plants or heavy clustered defenders  

### Defending
- Use smokes to slow attacker advances  
- Incendiary in chokepoints to block rotations  
- Orbital Strike can delay or eliminate rushes  
- Stim Beacon to support teammates holding sites  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy **strategic utility placement**  
- Controllers who manage team vision and map control  
- Those who like supporting pushes and post-plant situations
