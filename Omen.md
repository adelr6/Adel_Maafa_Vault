## General Presentation

- Role: [[Controller 
- Origin: Unknown  
- Playstyle: Vision denial / Map control  
- Strengths: Smokes, teleportation, stealth, disorienting enemies  

Omen is a mysterious controller who manipulates shadows to control sightlines, disorient opponents, and create confusion for his team.

## How to Play Omen

### Attacking
- Use **Dark Cover** to block sightlines for site entries  
- **Shrouded Step** for aggressive repositioning or flanking  
- **Paranoia** to blind defenders and initiate pushes  
- **From the Shadows** to gather intel, split sites, or clutch  

### Defending
- Place smokes to delay pushes and control angles  
- Teleport to surprise enemies or rotate quickly  
- Use **Paranoia** on choke points to slow enemy advances  
- Ultimate can secure rotations or dislodge attackers  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy **stealth, map manipulation, and disorientation**  
- Controllers who provide both offensive and defensive utility  
- Those who like unpredictable movement and high utility impact
