#  Pearl

Pearl is a Valorant map featuring **three main lanes** with a central Mid area. The map emphasizes **long-range fights, strategic positioning, and coordinated pushes**. Teams need to control Mid to influence rotations and site takes effectively.

---

## General Presentation

- Number of Sites: 2 (A & B)
- Special Feature: Central Mid control, long sightlines
- Typical Playstyle: Tactical, ranged control, strategic rotations
- Good for: Duelists, Controllers, Initiators, supportive Sentinels

---

## Key Areas of the Map

### Site A
- Wide open spaces
- Long sightlines favoring mid-to-long range weapons
- Good for coordinated utility pushes

### Site B
- Slightly tighter angles
- More verticality than A
- Useful for aggressive flanking plays

### Mid
- Central area connecting both sites
- Critical for rotations and controlling the flow of the game
- Holds multiple high-ground positions

---

## Strategic Approach

### Attacking on Pearl
- Take early control of Mid to split defender focus
- Use utility to clear long sightlines
- Coordinate pushes with a combination of aggressive and supportive agents

Recommended attackers:
- [[Phoenix]]  
- [[Raze]]  
- [[Skye]]  

---

### Defending on Pearl
- Hold angles from Mid and sites carefully
- Use utility to delay pushes
- Rotate smartly to cover both sites

Strong defenders:

- [[Sage]]  
- [[Cypher]]
