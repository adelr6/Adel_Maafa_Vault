## General Presentation

- Role: [[Initiator]]
- Origin: Turkey  
- Playstyle: Information-gathering / Entry setup  
- Strengths: Recon, crowd control, post-plant support  

Fade specializes in revealing enemy positions and controlling the flow of fights. She can disorient opponents, mark them for teammates, and force them out of hiding spots.

---

## Abilities

| Icon               | Ability   | Type      | Description                                                                                                           |
| ------------------ | --------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[Seize.png]]     | Seize     | Basic     | Throws a tether that attaches to the first enemy hit, impairing their movement and hearing.                           |
| ![[haunt.png]]     | Haunt     | Basic     | Sends out a ghostly tracker that passes through walls, revealing enemies caught in its path.                          |
| ![[prowler.png]]   | Prowler   | Signature | Releases a controllable creature that homes in on enemies, applying debuffs on contact.                               |

---

## How to Play Fade

### Attacking
- Use Seize to lock down key choke points  
- Haunt gives information about enemy positioning  
- Prowler clears angles or forces rotations  
- Nightfall opens sites for safe entries or post-plant control

### Defending
- Delay pushes with Seize and Prowler  
- Reveal enemies with Haunt before fights  
- Ultimate can deny site retakes or clutch situations

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who like information-gathering  
- Coordinated team players  
- Initiators who enjoy setting up plays
