## General Presentation

- Role: [[Sentinel]]
- Origin: France  
- Playstyle: Precision / Defensive lockdown  
- Strengths: Long-range picks, area control, traps  

Chamber is a tactical sentinel who combines precise firearms, traps, and teleportation to control sites and deny enemies space.

## How to Play Chamber

### Attacking
- Use **Headhunter** to pick off enemies from long-range  
- **Trademark** to slow pushes and control chokepoints  
- **Rendezvous** for fast repositioning or flanking  
- **Tour De Force** to secure site control or clutch situations  

### Defending
- Place **Trademarks** to cover entrances and delay pushes  
- Use **Rendezvous** for mobility across sites  
- Sniping with **Headhunter** or **Tour De Force** to punish overextensions  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy precision and tactical positioning  
- Sentinels who combine mobility, traps, and long-range damage  
- Strategic defenders controlling multiple angles
