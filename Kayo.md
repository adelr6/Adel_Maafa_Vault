## General Presentation

- Role: [[Initiator]]  
- Origin: Machine (Constructed in Germany)  
- Playstyle: Suppression / Aggression control  
- Strengths: Ability suppression, entry disruption, coordinated team plays  

KAY/O excels at disabling enemy abilities while helping his team push aggressively. His kit allows him to control space and force fights on favorable terms.

## How to Play KAY/O

### Attacking
- Use ZERO/point to suppress enemy defenders before entry  
- Flash/drive to initiate fights  
- FRAG/ment to clear corners or force opponents out of positions  
- NULL/cmd to lock down sites and prevent abilities from turning fights  

### Defending
- Suppress enemies pushing with ZERO/point  
- Use flashes to stall or control site  
- FRAG/ment for area denial  
- NULL/cmd to deny post-plant retakes

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who like coordinated team plays  
- Those who enjoy controlling enemy abilities  
- Initiators who prefer a mix of aggression and support
