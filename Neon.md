## General Presentation

- Role: [[Duelist]]
- Origin: Philippines  
- Playstyle: Ultra-aggressive / Mobility focused  
- Strengths: Speed, space creation, fast entries  

Neon specializes in raw speed and instant map control. She sprints, slides, and bursts lightning damage, allowing her to enter sites faster than any other agent.

## How to Play Neon

### Attacking
- Use High Gear to rush and surprise defenders  
- Slide to reposition during duels  
- Fast Lane blocks vision and opens instant entry paths  
- Relay Bolt helps disable defenders holding angles  

### Defending
- Take aggressive first picks  
- Rotate extremely fast thanks to speed  
- Concuss enemies to deny site control  
- Overdrive can stop full pushes alone

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who like going first into site  
- Fast decision-makers  
- Duelists who rely on movement and timing
