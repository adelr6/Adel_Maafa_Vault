## General Presentation

- Role: [[Duelist]]
- Origin: United Kingdom  
- Playstyle: Self-sufficient / Aggressive  
- Strengths: Self-heal, flashes, strong entry potential  

Phoenix is built for confident players who like taking duels. He can flash, heal himself, control space with fire, and even get a second chance with his ultimate.

---

## Abilities

| Icon                 | Ability   | Type                                                                                                             | Description                                                                                    |
| -------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[Hot_Hands.png]]   | Hot Hands | Basic                                                                                                            | Throws a fireball that explodes after a delay or impact, damaging enemies and healing Phoenix. |
| ![[Blaze.png]]       | Blaze     | Basic                                                                                                            | Creates a wall of fire blocking vision that damages enemies and heals Phoenix.                 |
| ![[Curveball.png]]   | Curveball | Signature                                                                                                        | A curved flash that blinds enemies around corners, perfect for entry fights.                   |
| ![[Run_it_Back.png]] | Ultimate  | Phoenix marks his location and respawns there if killed during the duration. A second life to take risky fights. |                                                                                                |

---

## How to Play Phoenix

### Attacking
- Use Curveball to flash corners before peeking  
- Blaze creates safe entry paths  
- Hot Hands clears close positions  
- Run It Back to take risky duels safely

### Defending
- Delay pushes with Blaze  
- Use flashes to retake sites  
- Heal mid-fight to stay alive  
- Ultimate to contest site control aggressively

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who like leading the push  
- Duelists who want independence  
- Beginners learning entry mechanics
