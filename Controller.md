# Role summary

**Controllers** are one of the four agent roles in Valorant. Their abilities allow them to help teammates by blocking enemy vision, taking control of areas, and forcing opponents to push through chokepoints.

The game has currently 6 Controller agents listed by chronological order of arrival : 
- [[Brimstone]]
- [[Viper]]
- [[Omen]] (personal favorite)
- [[Astra]] (personal favorite)
- [[Harbor]]
- [[Clove]]
