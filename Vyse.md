## General Presentation

- Role: [[Sentinel]]
- Origin: Radiant / Mixed Origin  
- Playstyle: Area control / Disruption  
- Strengths: Traps, slow zones, weapon disruption  

Vyse is a metallic mastermind who uses liquid metal to isolate, trap, and disarm her enemies. She sets up traps and utilizes unique utility to slow, blind, and disrupt opponents on both attack and defense. 

---

## Abilities

| Icon                  | Ability      | Type      | Description                                                                                                                         |
| --------------------- | ------------ | --------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ![[shear.png]]        | Shear        | Basic     | Places hidden filaments of liquid metal. When triggered, an indestructible wall bursts from the ground to block or isolate enemies. |
| ![[arc_rose.png]]     | Arc Rose     | Signature | Places a stealth Arc Rose. Activate it to blind enemies looking at it, then pick it up and redeploy later.                          |
| ![[razorvine.png]]    | Razorvine    | Basic     | Throws a nest of liquid metal that becomes invisible on landing. Activate it to create a slowing and damaging thorn zone.           |
| ![[steel_garden.png]] | Steel Garden | Ultimate  | Sends out a torrent of metal thorns that jam enemies’ primary weapons after a brief windup.                                         |

---

## How to Play Vyse

### Attacking
- Use Shear to isolate defenders before entry  
- Place Arc Rose early to blind defenders during pushes  
- Razorvine to slow choke points  
- Steel Garden to disrupt enemy retakes or trades

### Defending
- Trap common entry points with Shear and Razorvine  
- Use Arc Rose to blind attackers pushing through narrow lanes  
- Steel Garden during post-plant situations to jam weapons

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who enjoy **trapping and zoning enemies**  
- Sentinels with disruptive and defensive utility  
- Those who like strategic placement over pure fragging
