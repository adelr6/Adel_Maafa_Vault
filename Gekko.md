## General Presentation

- Role: [[Initiator]]  
- Origin: USA  
- Playstyle: Crowd control / Entry support  
- Strengths: Utility harassment, site control, recon  

Gekko specializes in sending out his creatures to harass enemies, gather information, and disrupt their positioning, making site entries safer for his team.

| Icon              | Ability  | Type      | Description                                                                                                |

---

## How to Play Gekko

### Attacking
- Send Mosh Pit or Wingman to clear corners and slow pushes  
- Use Dizzy to control enemy movement and vision  
- Thrash for post-plant pressure or breaking defensive setups  

### Defending
- Hold angles while deploying creatures to delay or reveal enemies  
- Mosh Pit and Wingman can punish aggressive entries  
- Dizzy and Thrash can support retakes  

---

## Recommended Playstyle

- Players who like controlling space  
- Supportive initiators  
- Those who enjoy utility-heavy play
